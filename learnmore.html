General Knowledge

What is a Creeper?

A Creeper is a common hostile mob that approaches players silently and explodes, destroying blocks and dealing massive damage. They were originally created by mistake in 2009 when Notch mixed up the height and length variables of a pig model.

What are Skeletons?

Skeletons are fast, undead hostile mobs armed with bows that shoot arrows at players and other targets. They burn under direct sunlight unless they are wearing head armor or standing in water/shade.

What does a crafting table do?

The Crafting Table is one of the most essential blocks in Minecraft. It expands the player's basic 2x2 crafting grid in the inventory into a 3x3 grid, allowing the creation of advanced tools, armor, weapons, and complex machinery.

What is Redstone used for?

Redstone acts like electrical wiring in Minecraft. It can transfer power between components, enabling players to automate farms, construct working doors, traps, and even build complex logic gates or fully functional computers inside the game.
